About Mac'Lurkin

Mac'Lurkin is an esports team forged from grit, instinct, and a shared love for competition. Founded by Renokas1 and TechnoGustav, we began as a small group of friends grinding late-night Valorant lobbies — and grew into a focused, ever-evolving team aiming for the top tiers of the competitive scene.

Our primary battleground is Valorant, where every round is a test of reflexes, communication, and trust. We don’t just play to win — we play to outsmart, outmaneuver, and outlast. Each of us brings a unique playstyle, but together, we form a cohesive unit that thrives under pressure and adapts in real time.

The name "Mac'Lurkin" represents more than a tag — it’s our mindset. It’s the quiet confidence before a clutch. The lurking flank that flips a round. The calm before the chaos. We believe in playing smart, striking fast, and always backing each other up — no matter the odds.

Beyond the game, Mac'Lurkin is a creative force. We produce content, support new talent, and build a growing community of players and fans who share our passion. Whether it’s tournaments, scrims, or late-night streams, we bring energy, personality, and a relentless drive to improve.

We’re not here to follow trends — we’re here to set them. And this is only the beginning.

— The Mac'Lurkin Team
